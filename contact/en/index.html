I sell my art exclusively via email, offering a personal and dedicated exchange. Art is an intimate encounter; I take the time to listen and create a bespoke piece that reflects your essence.

Some collectors entrust me with sourcing the perfect frame for their painting, others are inspired by an existing work and commission a unique piece that echoes its spirit while reflecting their own vision. I value meaningful dialogue over a simple “add to cart” approach; every interaction is part of the creation itself.

I warmly invite you to contact me at chaitealatte.art@gmail.com. My prices are fixed, ranging from €50 to €3000. Whether you are ready to acquire a piece or simply wish to exchange ideas, I look forward to our conversation.

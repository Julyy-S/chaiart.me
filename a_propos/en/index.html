Hello, I'm Chai, a 30-year-old self-taught artist.

For me, art is both an outlet and a refuge. Painting allows me to focus, to channel my emotions, and to transform them into tangible forms on canvas. Rather than striving for realism, I explore the expression of inner feelings and states of mind.

I create surreal, organic landscapes and hybrid works at the crossroads of painting and sculpture. Each piece invites multiple interpretations, allowing every viewer to project their own emotions and stories onto it.

My art plays with the boundaries between dream and reality, surface and volume, offering immersive and singular experiences.
